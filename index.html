MON-5 · Passive Income — Honestly Vetted

None of these are truly "passive" in year 1. All are part-time jobs that may become semi-passive in years 2-3. The unifying 2025-2026 catch across all four: AI-generated competition is flooding every market below.

1. Drum sample packs & loop libraries

Why it fits you specifically: 20 years of drumming is the rare ingredient. A real drummer's one-shots, fills, and grooves are something bedroom producers can't replicate. iOS/RN skills let you self-host on Gumroad/Stripe with automated delivery.

Setup: 1 pack = 2-4 weekends of recording + editing (50-100 sounds, key/tempo tagged). 6-12 months to get 3-5 packs live across platforms.

Realistic income: A well-marketed kit earns $500-$5,000 in month 1, then $50-$200/month tail. A producer with 5-10 packs across platforms: ~$200-$2,000/month after the catalog matures. Splice anecdotally ~$0.10-$0.50/download.

The catch: "Well-marketed" is doing huge lifting in those numbers. Most packs without social/YouTube promotion earn under $100 lifetime. Drums are the most saturated category — every producer needs them, every drummer sells them. Splice payouts have trended down as the subscriber pool grew faster than the payout pool. AI sample generators are accelerating the squeeze.

2. Niche indie iOS app portfolio ($2-10/mo subscriptions)

Why it fits you specifically: You already have RN/Expo skills and a shipped app. The 2025-2026 indie iOS playbook isn't big swings — it's small, focused B2B-leaning utilities (network tools, monitoring dashboards, log readers, on-call helpers) where your IT background gives you domain insight consumer-app builders lack.

Setup: 2-4 months per app. Realistic strategy: ship 3-5 small apps over 12-18 months, kill the ones that don't get traction, double down on what sticks. $99/yr Apple dev fee is the only hard cost.

Realistic income: Median indie iOS app earns under $100/month. The viral "$602k from a portfolio" stories are top-1% outliers. Competent solo dev shipping 3-5 apps and iterating: $200-$3,000 MRR in 18 months; $5-10K MRR achievable if one hits.

The catch: Not passive — apps need OS-update maintenance every 6-12 months (iOS deprecations, Expo SDK upgrades), customer support, ongoing ASO tweaks. Subscription churn is brutal in consumer; B2B is more stable but smaller TAM. AI app builders are flooding the App Store with low-quality clones, killing discovery.
