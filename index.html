§2 — ADHD Strategies: Exercise & Mindful Eating

Exercise: Starting When You Really Don't Want To

  • The Stupid-Simple Backup Rule. Define a floor workout that always counts — even 10 minutes of walking. ADHD brains stall on the gap between planned vs. possible. Close that gap to zero. Action creates motivation; waiting for it first doesn't work. Inflow
  • Immediate Concrete Reward. Pair exercise with something specific you enjoy right after — a specific coffee, episode, or snack. ADHD dopamine runs on near-future rewards. "Future health" is invisible to the system. Make the payoff real and now. NeuroLaunch
  • Body Doubling for Workouts. A Focusmate session, group class, or a walking phone call provides external accountability that compensates for the internal executive function deficit. External structure reliably beats willpower for ADHD brains. Shimmer

Mindful Eating: Working With the Dopamine-Seeking Brain

  • The 20-Second Pause. Before eating anything, take 3–5 deep breaths. Interrupts the automatic boredom-eating/stimulation-seeking loop. ADHD eating is often hijacked by dopamine hunger, not actual hunger — a tiny pause exposes which one it is. ADDitude
  • Protein + Fiber + Fat Combos. Meals with all three slow the dopamine crash cycle. Ultra-processed spike/crash loops amplify ADHD symptom swings — the food dysregulation and brain dysregulation feed each other. Jackie Silver Nutrition
